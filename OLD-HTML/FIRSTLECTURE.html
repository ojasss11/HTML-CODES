Procedure to prepare maggi:

  1. Boil water in a pot and add the broken Maggi noodles. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Drain the cooked noodles using a strainer and rinse them under cold running water.
  3. Empty the Maggi masala packet into a separate bowl.
  4. Return the drained noodles to the pot and add the Maggi masala. Mix well.
  5. Optional: Add sautéed vegetables, scrambled eggs, or cooked chicken as desired.
  6. Mix everything together and transfer the Maggi to a serving bowl or plate.
  7. Garnish with additional vegetables or herbs if desired, then serve.
